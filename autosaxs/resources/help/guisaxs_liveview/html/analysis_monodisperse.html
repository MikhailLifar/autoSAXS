Monodisperse analysis

p(r)

fit_distances (AUTORG + GNOM). Wizard options: fit_distances/fit_distances.conf. Per-TIFF results: fit_distances/<stem>/ — fit PNG, p(r) plot, GNOM .out.

DAM

Same GNOM setup as p(r), then fit_dammif (FAST) on the GNOM output. Additional folder: dammif/<stem>/ — bead model, CIF, 3D preview.

Primitives

fit_bodies (BODIES shapes). Shape list: fit_bodies/fit_bodies.conf. Per-TIFF: fit_bodies/<stem>/ — CSV, fit PNGs, 3D for best shape.
